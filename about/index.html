The story

Why I started this.

I've been building and running businesses since the late '90s — domain investing since 2001, ad sales, e-commerce, a shaving brand I launched on Shopify, and eventually a private performance ad network I built from scratch to 2 billion monthly impressions across 150+ countries.

Along the way I also became a PizzaForno franchise owner in Guelph. If you've seen the viral pizza vending machine at the University of Guelph — that's mine. The doorbell stunt was my idea.

Running all of those businesses meant dealing with every website and tech problem in the book. Broken forms. Contact pages that sent leads nowhere. Sites that ranked for nothing. Booking systems that didn't exist. I didn't learn how to fix these things in a course — I learned them because I was losing money when they didn't work.

On top of that, I've spent thousands of hours learning AI — not to talk about it, but to actually use it. I know what it can do for a small business right now, what it can't, and how to build it so it runs in the background and makes your operation smarter without you having to think about it. You shouldn't have to spend years figuring that out yourself.

What I'm here to do is simple: fix the tech that's been holding your business back, and automate as much of the repetitive work as we can so you can focus on what actually matters. You tell me what's broken or what's eating your time — I'll handle the rest.

Overhauled.ai is a one-person operation. You talk to me, I do the work, and I stand behind it. No junior contractors, no hand-offs, no runaround.
