FAQ

Common questions. Plain answers.

What's the difference between the three packages?

The Lead Leak Audit ($297) is the starting point, we find and fix the specific things losing you leads right now. The Lead Machine (from $2,997) is the full transformation: new or rebuilt site, AI that responds to enquiries instantly, and automated follow-up. The Autopilot Plan (from $397/mo) keeps it all improving month after month. Most clients start with the Audit and upgrade once they see how much was being missed.

Do I need to know anything about tech?

Not a thing. You tell me what you need in plain English, I handle everything technical. You don't need to know what hosting, DNS, or SSL means. That's my job.

How much does The Lead Machine actually cost?

Starting from $2,997 depending on scope. Whether it's a new build or a rebuild, and how complex the AI automation needs to be, you'll get a firm flat-rate quote before anything starts. If you already did the $297 Audit, that comes off the price.

What's an AI agent and do I actually need one?

An AI agent is software that does the job a person would normally do, 24 hours a day, without forgetting, without getting tired. The simplest version: someone fills out your form at 11pm and gets a personalised reply in under a minute, written in your voice. More advanced uses: following up on quotes that went quiet, answering the 10 questions customers always ask before they buy, booking appointments, collecting Google reviews after a job is done. If leads are slipping through the cracks or you're spending time on repetitive back-and-forth, it pays for itself fast.

How long does a project take?

Free check: within 24 hours. Lead Leak Audit: 48 hours. Lead Machine: under 2 weeks. Autopilot Plan is ongoing. Faster is possible if it's urgent, just ask.

What if something breaks after you're done?

Every project includes 30 days of free support. After that, the Autopilot Plan retainer covers ongoing fixes, updates, and support. Or you carry on independently, you own everything.

Do I own my website when it's done?

100%. Domain, files, content, AI agents, you own all of it. I don't hold anything hostage. If you ever want to move on, you take everything with you.

Do you work with businesses outside Canada?

Yes, everything is done remotely so location doesn't matter. I work with businesses across Canada and internationally. Most clients never need to meet in person.
