FAQ

Common questions. Plain answers.

Do I need to know anything about tech?

Not a thing. You tell me what you need in plain English — I handle everything technical. You don't need to know what hosting, DNS, or SSL means. That's my job.

How much does a new website actually cost?

Most small business sites land between $800 and $2,500 depending on size and features. You'll get a firm flat-rate quote before anything starts — no surprise invoices.

What's an "AI agent" and do I actually need one?

An AI agent is software that handles a job a person would normally do — 24 hours a day, without forgetting, without getting tired. The simplest example: someone fills out your contact form at 11pm and gets a personalized reply in under a minute, written in your voice.

Other things small businesses use them for: following up on quotes that never got a response, sending appointment reminders and rebook prompts, answering the 10 questions customers always ask before they buy, collecting Google reviews after a job is done, or flagging leads that need a human to step in.

Most small businesses don't need one on day one — but the ones that have one tend to respond faster, follow up more consistently, and close more of what they already have coming in. If leads are falling through the cracks or you're spending time on repetitive back-and-forth, it's worth a conversation.

How long does a project take?

Fixes: typically 24–48 hours. New websites: 2–3 business days. AI agents: 1–2 days depending on complexity. Faster is possible if it's urgent — just ask.

What if something breaks after you're done?

Every project includes 30 days of free support. After that, you can carry on independently or stay on a monthly retainer ($500+) for ongoing coverage.

Do I own my website when it's done?

100%. Domain, files, content — you own all of it. I don't hold anything hostage. If you ever want to move on, you take everything with you.

Can you fix what I have instead of rebuilding?

Often, yes. I'll look at your site honestly and tell you whether a fix is the right call. Rebuilding only makes sense if the existing site is genuinely beyond saving.

Do you work with businesses outside Guelph?

Yes — everything is done remotely so location doesn't matter. I work with businesses across Ontario and beyond. Most clients never need to meet in person.
