Common Questions

Straight Answers.
No Tech Jargon.

Do We need to know anything about tech?

Not even a little. You tell us what you need in plain English — We handle everything technical. You don't need to know what hosting, DNS, or SSL means. That's our job.

What if We don't have a domain or website yet?

No problem at all — that's actually the easiest situation to start from. We'll help you pick a good domain name, register it, set up hosting, and build everything from scratch.

How much does a new website actually cost?

Most small business and trades websites come in between $800 and $2,500 depending on size and features. You'll get a firm flat-rate quote before anything starts — no surprises.

How long does it take?

Website fixes are typically done within 24–48 hours. New websites are usually live within 5–7 business days. Faster is possible if it's urgent — just ask.

What if something breaks after you're done?

Every project comes with a 30-day support window at no extra charge. If something goes sideways in the first month, We'll fix it. If you want ongoing coverage after that, monthly retainers start at $250.

Do We own my website when it's done?

100%. You own everything — the domain, the files, the content. We don't hold anything hostage. If you ever want to move on or work with someone else, you can take everything with you.

We already have a website. Can you fix it instead of rebuilding?

Absolutely. Sometimes a fix is all you need. We'll look at what you have and give you an honest answer — rebuild only makes sense if the existing site is beyond saving or costs more to fix than replace.

Do you work with businesses outside Guelph?

Yes — everything is done remotely so location doesn't matter. We work with businesses across Ontario, Canada, and internationally. Most clients never need to meet in person.
