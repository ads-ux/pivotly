Privacy Policy

How Overhauled.ai collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: May 2026

Who We Are

Overhauled.ai is a web services business operated by Mark Hurson, based in Guelph, Ontario, Canada. We build and fix websites and AI systems for small businesses worldwide. You can reach us at hello@overhauled.ai or by phone at (519) 942-5698.

What Information We Collect

We only collect information you give us directly. This happens when you:

  • Fill out the contact form on our website
  • Send us an email
  • Contact us by phone

The information typically includes your name, email address, business name, phone number (if provided), and a description of what you need help with.

We also use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymous usage data like pages visited and time on site. No personally identifiable information is collected through Google Analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Send you a project proposal or invoice if you become a client

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

How We Store Your Information

Contact form submissions are processed via Netlify Forms and delivered to our email inbox. We may store your contact details in a simple spreadsheet or email folder to track project conversations. Email correspondence is stored in our Google Workspace account, which is subject to Google's privacy and security policies.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses Google Analytics, which sets cookies to track anonymous visitor behaviour. These cookies do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, or use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We also use the Meta Pixel (Facebook Pixel) to measure the performance of any advertising we run on Meta platforms. The Meta Pixel may set cookies and collect information about your visit, including pages viewed and actions taken. It does not collect your name or contact details unless you've interacted with a Meta ad. You can opt out of Meta's data collection via Meta's ad preferences or through your browser's cookie settings.

Your Rights (PIPEDA & Canadian Privacy Law)

As a Canadian business, we operate under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request a copy of that information
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your information

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@overhauled.ai. We'll respond within 30 days.

Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to complete your project and for a reasonable period afterward (typically up to two years) in case you need to refer back to past work. If you'd like your information removed sooner, just ask.

Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on Netlify. Form submissions are processed by Netlify Forms. Analytics are provided by Google Analytics. We also use the Meta Pixel for advertising measurement. Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data on our behalf.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
