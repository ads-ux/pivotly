Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overhauled.ai ("we", "our", or "us") is a web design and digital marketing service based in Guelph, Ontario. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — your name, email address, and phone number when you fill out our contact form or book a call.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, and browser/device type, collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Communications — messages you send us through our contact form or by email.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide our services
  • To send project updates and invoices (only to existing clients)
  • To improve our website based on usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. This uses cookies to collect anonymized data such as pages visited and session duration. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We also use the Meta Pixel to measure the effectiveness of any advertising we run on Facebook and Instagram. No personally identifiable information is shared with Meta through this pixel.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights (PIPEDA)

As a Canadian business, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data collection
  • Request deletion of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@overhauled.ai.

Third-Party Services

Our website may link to third-party services including Calendly (for booking calls). These services have their own privacy policies and we are not responsible for their practices.

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Our site is served over HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were last made.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@overhauled.ai.
